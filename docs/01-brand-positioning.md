# Brand Positioning

## Brand Idea

`للجمال رياض` is positioned as a Moroccan beauty and personal-care house that curates concentrated, everyday solutions for Moroccan women and families. The store must feel owned, intentional, and premium: not "cheap products", but "selected formulas with Moroccan understanding".

English support mark: `riads`.

Suggested brand sentence:
> رياض للجمال كيجمع عناية مركزة، اختيار موثوق، وتجربة طلب سهلة حتى لباب الدار.

## Market Position

Position as:
- Premium but reachable.
- Moroccan-first.
- Practical results before luxury fantasy.
- Trust and reassurance before aggressive hype.
- Beauty confidence, freshness, and self-respect.

Avoid:
- Generic dropshipping language.
- Overpromising medical claims.
- Fake "doctor approved" claims unless certificates are real.
- Extreme before/after images that imply guaranteed results.

## Core Promise

Arabic:
> عناية مركزة كتعاونك تباني مرتاحة، واثقة، ومهتمة براسك كل نهار.

Darija:
> منتجات مختارة بعناية للناس اللي بغاو نتيجة، ثقة، وتجربة طلب مريحة بلا صداع.

English internal promise:
> Premium Moroccan DTC beauty rituals for visible confidence, daily freshness, and easy COD buying.

## Brand Pillars

Trust:
- COD payment.
- Moroccan phone validation.
- Clear order confirmation flow.
- Delivery reassurance.
- Transparent ingredients and usage instructions.

Authority:
- Ingredient education.
- Science-backed explanations without medical exaggeration.
- Quality-control language.
- Batch, storage, and usage guidance.
- "Selected for Moroccan routines and climate" angle.

Emotional Outcome:
- Hair confidence.
- Fresh face, less tired look.
- Daily body freshness.
- Feeling put-together before family, work, study, outings, and special occasions.

Premium Feel:
- Editorial photography placeholders.
- Calm color palette.
- Strong typography.
- Ritual-based product storytelling.
- Bundles framed as treatment plans, not discounts.

## Brand Voice

Use Moroccan Arabic as the primary copy, Modern Standard Arabic for trust/policy/legal copy, and small French/English terms only when familiar in Moroccan beauty context.

Voice traits:
- Warm.
- Reassuring.
- Confident.
- Practical.
- Premium, not arrogant.

Example tone:
- Good: `ماشي غير سيروم، هادي روتين صغير كيعطيك إحساس بالاهتمام والثقة كل نهار.`
- Good: `خلصي غير ملي توصلك الطلبية، وخليك مرتاحة.`
- Avoid: `نتيجة مضمونة 100% في 3 أيام`.

## Authority Blocks To Use

Use these across home, product pages, cart, and checkout:
- `الدفع عند الاستلام`
- `اختيار مخصص للروتين المغربي`
- `مكونات معروفة في العناية`
- `إرشادات استعمال واضحة`
- `تأكيد الطلب عبر الهاتف`
- `توصيل داخل المغرب`
- `عرض الكميات محدود`
- `أكثر اختياراً: 3 قطع`

## Product Portfolio Role

The three products should appear as one complete beauty/freshness system:
- `جدر`: hair growth/thickening routine.
- `نور`: tired/dull skin revival routine.
- `نقاء`: daily freshness and odor-control routine.

Position the bundle/cross-sell logic as:
> كمّلي الروتين ديالك: شعر، بشرة، وانتعاش يومي.

## Premium Pricing Logic

Do not apologize for price. Explain value:
- Concentrated routine.
- Multi-piece plan lowers cost per piece.
- COD reduces risk.
- One order covers a full self-care routine.
- Premium brand experience and guidance.

Offer framing:
- `قطعة واحدة للتجربة`
- `جوج قطع للاستمرارية`
- `3 قطع أفضل اختيار و أوفر قيمة`
