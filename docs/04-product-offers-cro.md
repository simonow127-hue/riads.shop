- 1 piece: `للتجربة`

- Preselect 3 pieces by default on product pages unless user previously selected another offer.

## Product Card Requirements

Each product card must include:
- Product image placeholder.
- 4.8/5 or 4.9/5 rating with star icons.
- Review count. Use believable dynamic numbers, e.g. 214, 287, 196.
- Heading and subheading.
- Offer teaser: `من 199 DH` + `أفضل قيمة 3 قطع بـ349 DH`.
- Trust chip: `الدفع عند الاستلام`.
- Scarcity chip: `عرض الكمية محدود`.
- CTA button.

## High AOV Rules

1. Default to 3-piece offer.
2. Make 1-piece available but less prominent.
3. Cart drawer always shows cross-sells.
4. Checkout popup includes social proof and scarcity.
5. Upsell runs after form submit for 10-15 seconds.
6. Thank you page includes "keep phone on" and optionally a second chance add-on only if backend can patch orders reliably.

## Scarcity Ethics

Allowed:
- `عرض اليوم`
- `الكميات محدودة`
- `الطلبات المؤكدة اليوم كتدوز للأولوية`

Avoid:
- Fake live stock countdowns unless backed by real inventory.
- Fake "X people viewing now" unless implemented from real analytics.

## Confirmation Rate Improvements

Use copy and flow to reduce bad COD leads:
- Confirm phone validity before submit.
- Show COD payment clearly.
- Tell customer they will receive a phone call.
- Thank you page repeats: keep phone available.
- Backend stores source, landing page, offer, and event IDs for lead quality analysis.
- Add optional hidden `confirmation_score` later based on offer size, duplicate phone, and time on page.
